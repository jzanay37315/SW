Campus Life





School Church

Was Established In 1978

In 1978, a beacon of faith and education was established with the founding of the School Church. This institution stands as a testament to the harmonious integration of spiritual and academic pursuits. Conceived as a haven for both intellectual and spiritual growth, the School Church has been a cornerstone in nurturing the minds and souls of generations. Since its inception, it has played a pivotal role in fostering a sense of community, instilling values, and promoting a holistic approach to learning. With each passing year, the echoes of hymns and the rustle of turning pages have woven a tapestry of knowledge and faith within the hallowed halls of this venerable institution. The year 1978 marked not just the birth of a school but the inception of a sacred space where education and spirituality intertwine, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts and minds of all who have passed through its doors.









School Library

Was Built In 1980

In the year 1980, a haven for knowledge and imagination was brought to life with the construction of the School Library. This architectural gem stands as a testament to the commitment of fostering a love for learning within the academic community. As the library doors swung open for the first time, a world of literary treasures unfolded within its walls. Students and educators alike found solace in the serene ambiance, surrounded by shelves laden with books that spanned genres and disciplines. The School Library quickly became the beating heart of intellectual curiosity, a space where minds could wander through the pages of history, science, literature, and more. Over the years, it has evolved into a dynamic hub for research, study sessions, and quiet contemplation. The class of 1980 not only witnessed the birth of a physical structure but the inception of a timeless repository of knowledge that continues to shape the intellectual landscape of the school community.









School Auditorium

Was Built In 1982

In 1982, the school proudly unveiled a new cornerstone of its cultural and artistic identity with the construction of the School Auditorium. This architectural marvel not only provided a grand venue for academic ceremonies but also became the vibrant stage for a myriad of performances and events. With its majestic architecture and state-of-the-art facilities, the auditorium quickly emerged as the beating heart of the school's artistic expression and communal gatherings. From theatrical productions and musical concerts to academic presentations, the auditorium served as a versatile space where students could showcase their talents and the community could come together in celebration. The class of 1982 witnessed the birth of an iconic space that transcended its physical structure, becoming a cherished center for the enrichment of both artistic endeavors and communal spirit within the school's legacy.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    Nestled within the heart of the school campus, the newly inaugurated school canteen stands as a vibrant hub of culinary delight, fostering a welcoming atmosphere for students and staff alike. Bathed in natural light streaming through large windows adorned with cheerful curtains, the canteen boasts a modern and open design, promoting a sense of community. Sleek counters showcase a diverse array of freshly prepared meals, from wholesome salads and nourishing wraps to indulgent treats that cater to varying tastes and dietary preferences. The aroma of savory delights wafts through the air, enticing hungry patrons to explore the menu crafted with a focus on both nutrition and flavor. Collaborative seating arrangements and cozy nooks encourage social interactions, transforming the canteen into a lively space where friendships are forged and ideas are exchanged. With its contemporary aesthetic, culinary diversity, and inviting ambiance, the new school canteen emerges as a central hub for not just meals but also a place for students to unwind and connect.





  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    In a transformative leap toward modern efficiency, our institution has seamlessly integrated an Online Attendance Management System, revolutionizing the traditional process of recording attendance. This innovative system harnesses cutting-edge technology to streamline and automate the attendance tracking process. Students and faculty now navigate a user-friendly online portal to mark their presence, providing a hassle-free alternative to the manual methods of the past. The system's robust features include real-time data synchronization, generating accurate and instantaneous attendance reports for administrative purposes. Furthermore, its adaptability allows for customization to suit the specific needs of different departments and courses. With this implementation, the institution has not only embraced technological advancement but has also significantly reduced the administrative burden associated with attendance tracking, fostering a more efficient and transparent approach to academic management.
